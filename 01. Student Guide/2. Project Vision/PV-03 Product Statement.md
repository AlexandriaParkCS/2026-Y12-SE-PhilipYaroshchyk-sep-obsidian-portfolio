A product statement communicates the intent of the application and the importance of the
project to all concerned personnel. Provide an overall statement summarising, at the highest level, the unique position the product intends to fill in the marketplace. The following format may be used:

For of
	\<target customer\>
who
	\<statement of the need or opportunity\>
the (product name) is a 
	\<product category\>
that
	\<statement of key benefits; that is, compelling reasons to use\>.
Unlike
	\<primary competitive alternatives\>
the (product name)
	\<statement of primary differentiation\>

Augment the statement with a product perspective that puts the product in perspective to other related products and the user’s environment. If the product is independent and totally self-contained, state it here. If the product is a component of a larger system, then this subsection needs to relate how these systems interact and needs to identify the relevant interfaces between the systems.

### Example <hr>

**For** students and young adults **who** struggle to track their spending, manage savings, and understand their financial habits, **the BudgetWise** application is a simple, youth‑friendly budgeting tool **that** provides clear transaction recording, real‑time dashboards, spending visualisations, and achievable goal‑tracking to build financial confidence.

**Unlike** complex, subscription‑based budgeting apps designed for adults with advanced financial needs, BudgetWise offers an accessible, intuitive, and educational platform tailored specifically to young users, focusing on clarity, ease of use, and meaningful financial insights.

BudgetWise is designed as a **stand‑alone, self‑contained budgeting application** that operates independently within a user’s personal environment. It does not rely on external financial systems, bank integrations, or third‑party APIs. Instead, it provides a controlled, educational space where users manually enter transactions, set goals, and view personalised financial summaries. This approach supports learning by encouraging users to actively engage with their financial data rather than relying on automated imports.

If integrated into a larger educational ecosystem, BudgetWise can operate as a complementary tool alongside classroom instruction, financial literacy programs, or wellbeing initiatives. In such contexts, the only required interface is the user’s web browser, ensuring compatibility and ease of deployment. No direct data exchange with school systems is required, preserving privacy and simplifying implementation.
