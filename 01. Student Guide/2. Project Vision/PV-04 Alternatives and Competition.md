### Example <hr>

Several budgeting applications currently exist, but most are designed for adults with complex financial needs. These include tools such as Pocketbook, Frollo, YNAB (You Need A Budget), and Mint. While these platforms offer powerful features—automatic bank syncing, advanced analytics, and multi‑account management, they often introduce unnecessary complexity for younger users. Many require subscriptions, contain cluttered interfaces, or assume a level of financial literacy that students may not yet possess.

BudgetWise differentiates itself by focusing specifically on youth financial literacy, offering a simple, guided, and educational experience. Unlike competitors that prioritise automation and advanced financial planning, BudgetWise emphasises manual entry, clear visual feedback, and goal‑oriented learning, helping young users build foundational budgeting skills. Its clean interface, minimal cognitive load, and school‑friendly design make it uniquely suited for students and first‑time budgeters.
