The problem of
	\<describe the problem here\>
affects
	\<the stakeholders affected by the problem>.
The impact of this problem is
	\<describe the impact of the problem\>.
A successful solution would be
	 \<list some key benefits of a successful solution\>.


### Example <hr>

**The problem of** young people struggling to manage their personal finances due to limited financial literacy, difficulty tracking daily spending, and the lack of simple, accessible budgeting tools **affects** students, young adults, educators, and families who rely on clear financial habits to support independence and wellbeing.

**The impact of this problem is** that young people often overspend, fail to meet savings goals, and lack visibility over their financial behaviour. This leads to poor money‑management habits, reduced confidence, and long‑term financial stress. Existing budgeting apps are often too complex, expensive, or cluttered with advanced features, creating barriers for young users who need straightforward guidance.

**A successful solution would be** a secure, youth‑friendly budgeting application that allows users to easily record transactions, view spending summaries, and track savings goals. It would provide clear visualisations, real‑time dashboards, intuitive categorisation, and a simple interface that encourages consistent engagement. The solution would empower young people to build healthy financial habits, improve decision‑making, and gain confidence in managing their money.
