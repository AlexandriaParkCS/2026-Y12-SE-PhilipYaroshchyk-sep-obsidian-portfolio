### Example<hr>

BudgetWise is a youth‑focused budgeting application designed to help students and young adults develop essential financial literacy skills. Existing budgeting tools are often complex, expensive, or designed for adults with advanced financial needs, making them unsuitable for younger users. BudgetWise addresses this gap by offering a simple, intuitive, and educational platform that enables users to record transactions, monitor spending patterns, and set achievable savings goals.

The system is fully self‑contained and operates within a user’s personal environment, requiring only a web browser and basic internet access. Its design emphasises clarity, ease of use, and active engagement, encouraging users to manually enter financial data to build stronger budgeting habits. Real‑time dashboards, category breakdowns, and goal‑tracking features provide immediate visual feedback that supports learning and informed decision‑making.

BudgetWise differentiates itself from competitors such as Pocketbook, Frollo, YNAB, and Mint by focusing on simplicity and education rather than automation and advanced analytics. Its youth‑friendly interface and educational purpose make it ideal for school environments and personal use. Overall, BudgetWise empowers young people to understand and manage their finances confidently, supporting long‑term financial wellbeing and independence.
