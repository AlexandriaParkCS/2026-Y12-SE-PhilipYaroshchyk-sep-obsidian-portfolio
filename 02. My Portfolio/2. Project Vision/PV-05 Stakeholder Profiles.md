### Primary Stakeholders
As discussed in the previous sections, **YourCatsAndDogs** has two primary end users: pet owners and pet sitters:

***Pet owners (End Users)***
- **Description**: Individuals and families that travel and own one or several pet animals (most likely cats and/or dogs) 
- **Needs**: Seamless safe and secure service provided by experienced and trustworthy pet carers while pet owners are away.
- **Success Criteria**: Ability to consistently organise high quality pet care with ease. Ongoing business engagement with carers.

***Pet carers (End Users)***
- **Description**: Individuals who love to provide animal care, appreciate being rewarded fairly for their high quality services and appreciate job security and stability created by the recurring business engagements.
- **Needs**: Safety, decent pay, job security. Good job is recognised and rewarded by having access to more opportunities. 
- **Success Criteria**: Job is financially rewarding, job is always safe and secure, there are ongoing business engagements with pet owners. 

### Secondary Stakeholders
Given that **YourCatsAndDogs** serves as a platform that enables, provides safety controls and facilitates mutually beneficial business engagement of pet owners and pet sitters, application development and project team take the secondary stakeholder role.

***Product and Design Team***
- **Description**: Professionals responsible for design and marketing of **YourCatsAndDogs**
- **Needs**: Clear business requirements, empowerment to make design and product decisions
- **Success Criteria**: Application has a clear product and design vision, it is successful in fulfilling the market needs 

***Engineering Team***
- **Description**: Professionals in charge of implementation and maintenance of the system (Project managers, Software Engineers, Security Professionals, etc).
- **Needs**: Clear technical requirements. Empowerment to make technical decisions to create and support the product that has scalable architecture and maintainable and extendable codebase.
- **Success Criteria**: Application is created using best design and code practices. It is secure and stable. New feature can be added fast without compromising security and scalability.
