There are several existing pet sitting apps already on the market. Those include **Meowtel**, **Cat in a Flat** and **TrustedHousesitters**.  They each provide different types of services, for example **Meowtel** and **Cat in a Flat** specialise in cats and can provide itemised services like food and water refresh, litterbox clean up, playing, etc. In contrast **TrustedHousesitters** cater for a wide range of pets including farm and exotic pets.

While above applications do a great job for organising immediately required pet care, their focus is not on building long term relationships, which we believe is a major gap that makes the overall experience less seamless, less secure and less safe for everyone.   

**YourCatsAndDogs** differentiates itself from all of the above by focusing on nurturing long term business relationships between pet owners and sitters, which is ensures by 3 key features coordinated by the app:
- Safe and secure home provided by the pet owners
- High quality services are provided by trusted and verified pet sitters
- Pet sitting providers are well compensated for their services, which ensures their long term commitment to **YourCatsAndDogs**
