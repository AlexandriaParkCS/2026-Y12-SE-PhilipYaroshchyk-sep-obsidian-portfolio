# User Guide — YourCatsAndDogs

**Registration:** From the home page, select "Register" in the menu and enter a username, email, and password, then submit the form; the account is created and you are taken to the Login page to sign in.

**Login:** Select "Login" in the menu and enter your username and password; on success you are taken to your profile page, where all of your pets, bookings, and messages can be managed.

**Updating your profile:** From your profile page, upload a new avatar image and update fields such as email, postcode, and your "about" section, then click "Update profile" to save the changes.

**Adding a pet:** On your profile page, fill in the "Add a pet" form with the pet's name, species, breed, and year of birth, then click "Add"; the pet appears in your "Your pets" section with these details.

**Adding a pet care type:** Open a pet's details page by clicking "Details" against its entry in "Your pets", then fill in the "Add new care type" form (type of care, schedule, and description) and click "Add" to attach it to that pet.

**Creating a booking:** From a pet's details page, fill in the "Create a new booking request" form with a start date, end date, and daily price, then click "Request booking"; the new booking is listed on that pet's page for a sitter to find and accept.

**Messaging:** Once a booking has been confirmed, owners can open the pet's details page, scroll to "Bookings", and click "Message sitter", while sitters can go to "My sittings" and click "Message owner"; either way, type a message and click "Send" to add it to the conversation.
